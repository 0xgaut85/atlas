# Atlas Statue Model

Place your `atlas.glb` or `atlas.gltf` file in this directory.

The model will be automatically loaded and displayed in the hero section.

**Required:**
- Filename: `atlas.glb` (preferred) or `atlas.gltf`
- Format: GLB or GLTF 2.0
- Size: < 10 MB recommended

**Optional Optimization:**
You can compress your model with Draco to reduce file size:
https://gltf-transform.dev/
